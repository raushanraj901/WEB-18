Meet Our Industry Mentors

Industry Mentors at Masai School are working professionals in top technology companies, who volunteer from their personal time to help, guide and support Masai students as they reach the end of their courses. Our mentors are some of the best coding experts in India who will meet with the students twice via Zoom, after they have finished and cleared Unit 4. These sessions usually last for 1 hour and are based on one of the topics given by the instructors. Addressing a wide variety of topics, our Industry Mentors come from a variety of backgrounds, but all of them work in or around technology.
